TAKE ACTION

Learn how you can take action today to prevent gun violence

Research from Amnesty International has shown that these four acts are crucial to making everyone safer

1

Passing federal gun laws to keep guns from falling in the hands of those likely to misuse them

2

Invest in violence-prevention programs led by impacted communities

3

Fund gun violence research to inform evidence-based policy solutions

4

Provide support, including mental and physical health care, for survivors of gun violence

Here are some ways you can yet involved and contribute to the acts above

Call your representatives

Contact your elected representatives and demand that they support and advocate for effective gun violence prevention legislation. Call your U.S. Senators and Representative via the U.S. Capitol Switchboard at 202-224-3121 and tell them that you SUPPORT a federal ban on assault weapons and high-capacity ammunition magazines
